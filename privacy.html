What we collect

HomeFlow does not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information. The Chi Profile and all assessment data you enter are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

When you run an assessment, your inputs are sent to Google Gemini's API solely to generate your reading. These inputs are not stored, logged, or used for training by the developer.

Subscriptions

Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase and subscription data in accordance with their own privacy policy. No payment information is ever handled directly by HomeFlow.

Push Notifications

If you opt in to Daily Practice reminders, a device push token is registered via Expo's notification infrastructure. This token is used solely to deliver notifications and is not associated with any personal data.

Data Retention and Deletion

All locally stored data (Chi Profile, journals, assessments) can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app. We have no server-side copy of your data to delete.

Contact

For privacy questions regarding HomeFlow, please contact us via the App Store listing or at homeflowapp.ca.

Privacy by design

Flow is intentionally minimal and private. The app operates entirely offline. All data — your visited Water Forms, scene preferences, and audio settings — is stored locally on your device only and never leaves it.

Flow contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no third-party integrations, and no network requests of any kind. There is nothing to collect, share, or delete.

Data Retention and Deletion

Uninstalling Flow removes all locally stored preferences. There is no server-side data associated with your use of the app.

For privacy questions regarding Flow, please contact us via the App Store listing or at homeflowapp.ca.
