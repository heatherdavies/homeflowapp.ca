Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At HomeFlow, we believe your home's energy and your personal intentions are private. Our app is designed with a privacy-first approach.

Data Privacy & Storage

HomeFlow is designed with a privacy-first approach. All data you enter – including assessment results, photos of your space, and your cure tracking – is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal data on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with third parties. Any processing required for your assessments is handled securely and does not involve the permanent storage of your personal data on external servers.

Photos

Photos you take or upload within HomeFlow are used solely to generate your energy assessment. They are transmitted securely for analysis and are not stored, shared, or used for any other purpose.

Your Control

Because your data is stored on your device, you have complete control over it. Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@homeflowapp.ca.
