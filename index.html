The Foundation

A living tradition,
brought home.

HomeFlow was lovingly crafted by a deeply skilled BTB Feng Shui practitioner. Its foundations rest on thousands of years of accumulated wisdom, distilled into BTB's current form. Every questionnaire, every prompt, every cure reflects that living tradition.

Unlike traditional approaches that rely on magnetic compasses and rigid structural rules, Black Sect Tantric Buddhist Feng Shui centers on the flow of Chi, the power of intention, and the living connection between an individual's environment and their inner self.

A BTB assessment is not a simple checklist. It is the complex interweaving of life area priorities, elemental relationships, environmental forces, spatial geometry, predecessor energy, and personal intention, all held simultaneously and read in relation to one another. HomeFlow was built to honor that complexity. The wisdom is ancient and practitioner-led. The synthesis is precise and personal.

Personalization

The Chi Profile.

Before any assessment begins, users create their Chi Profile: a deeply personal intake that maps their intentions, life priorities, space history, elemental tendencies, and the areas of life calling most urgently for attention.

This profile is the heart of HomeFlow's personalization. Every reading is uniquely weighted to the user's stated goals, ensuring that cures feel privately crafted rather than generically prescribed. The user drives the experience entirely through their own inputs and observations; a language model then assists in synthesizing the rich interplay of all these threads into a cohesive reading, in the spirit and language of BTB.

Daily Practice

Feng Shui as a
sustained rhythm.

HomeFlow extends far beyond one-time assessments. Its Daily Practice ecosystem transforms Feng Shui from a weekend project into a living, breathing part of everyday life.

🌿
Seasonal & Monthly Themes

The practice is anchored in the Wu Xing (Five Elements) and the rhythm of the seasons. Each month carries its own theme and focus, guiding attention to the life areas most alive in that moment of the year.

☽
Weekly Arcs

Each week follows an energetic arc from Initiation (Monday and Tuesday) through Elevation (Wednesday to Friday) to Deep Yin (Sunday), with daily prompts tailored to the day's energy: Wealth Wednesday, Flow Friday, Sacred Saturday.

✦
Reflection & Journaling

A dedicated, timestamped journal offers rotating daily prompts to help users track the subtle shifts in their home's energy and their own inner state. "What did your home ask of you today?" A history of past practices is always accessible.
